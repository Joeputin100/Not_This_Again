Phrases ride Der Hölle Rache and escalate: gentle arcs → rising runs → the staccato high-F zig-zag finale. A clean trace = your posse out-sings her and she takes a hit; a sloppy/late trace = she belts a high note and thins your posse. (The Papageno "Pa-pa-pa" duet earlier in the canyon teaches this with easy repeated contours.)

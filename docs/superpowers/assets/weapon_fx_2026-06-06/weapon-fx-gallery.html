Weapon-FX Gallery — candy-Western gunfire options

Candidate fire visuals to bring the other weapons up to (or past) the polish of FROSTBITE and the Rainbow Prism Chain — the two shipped flagship effects (bottom row, for comparison). Every tile here is animated live with additive compositing (the same trick that makes FROSTBITE read as premium) and is designed to be shippable on Android with only additive 2D-canvas overlays, CPUParticles3D, and additive sprites — no custom spatial shaders (those white-rect on our mobile renderer). Each tile's small note says how it maps to an in-engine build, and the corner tag shows which weapon it suits.
